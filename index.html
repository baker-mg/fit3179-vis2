Earlier More Crowded Climbs

The vast majority of climbs occur during the Spring climbing season. Climbers tend to jump on the first suitable day during their stay to summit Mount Everest. This leads to a crowded summit and long queues in the Death Zone, the section of the climb above 8000 metres. A record 314 climbers summitted the mountain on the 23rd of May 2019.
